Hello! My Name is Aleksandr Sinayev.

But you can call me Aleks.

I am a fifth year graduate student in Quantitative Psychology at The Ohio State University. I study human decision making using predictive and mathematical modeling and machine learning. I am interested in using these methods to understand the way people think and to shape the environment in a way that makes effective decision making easier. I have studied both trivial choices that add up over time, like which product to buy, and consequential one-shot choices, like whether to save for retirement. Some presentation formats can increase both comprehension and desirable behavior. I also take pride in my ability to convey my findings to interested parties with little or no technical training.
